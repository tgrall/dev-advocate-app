Activity Management Application

This application is used to capture activity of the Developer Advocates, Solution Architects when they meet our community. This will allow us how much people we talk to, meet...

This is also a good place to organize participation to conferences: which ones to sponsor, or talk to. This application is also used to manage talks and organize Call For Papers responses.

This application is still under development and many features are incomplete or missing. Stay tuned!
